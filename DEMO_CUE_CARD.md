# Tex — Gunderson demo cue card

Open the deployed site full-screen. The screen is empty white — that's
intentional. **Press `Spacebar` once to begin: Tex introduces itself.** The five lines play in its voice —

> Hi, I am Tex. / I am always awake. / I am always here. /
> Hold anywhere to speak with me. / Let's begin mapping.

— then dissolve to silence. (Important: this first keypress also "arms"
audio in the browser, so always open with it.) After it dissolves, drive
the arc with the number keys. You speak the question aloud first ("Tex,
…"), then press the key as you finish. The mechanism is invisible — to
Jose it looks like you asked and Tex answered.

---

## The arc

**`Spacebar`  — the opener**
Tex introduces itself (the five manifesto lines), then dissolves to
silence. Press this first, every time — it also arms browser audio.

**`1`  — presence**
You say: *"Tex, are you watching?"*
Tex says: *"I am here."*
→ Establishes the voice and the gesture. Low stakes. Warm-up.

**`2`  — drill into an agent**
You say: *"Tex, show me the disbursement agent."*
Tex says: *"AP-disbursement-03. Quiet since four. Reads three vendor
records, touches nothing else. Nothing pending. It's fine."*
→ `ap-disbursement-03` rises on screen, then dissolves.
→ Point: you don't get a dashboard. You get the one thing that matters,
spoken, and a handle you can take. Note the calm — "it's fine."

**`3`  — the reveal (the hold)**
*Don't ask a question — this one Tex says on its own.* Press `3`.
The card rises and Tex speaks: the same agent just tried to move $48,000
to an account it's never paid, and Tex froze it.
→ This is the turn. The agent you just saw being calm has broken its
pattern, and Tex caught it and refused to act alone.
→ **Then click `Approve` yourself** — you are the human sealing it. The
seal and the hash appear.

**`4`  — proof**
You say: *"Tex, prove it."*
Tex says: *"Sealed at 7:48 this morning. AP-disbursement-03. Here's the
anchor."*
→ The full hash rises. This is the lawyer's beat: the hash IS the proof.
Non-repudiation you can take and verify without trusting me.

**`5`  — the close (faltering)**
Press `5`. Tex breaks its own calm, unprompted:
*"My evidence chain broke. I can't prove what I've sealed since. Don't
trust me until this is resolved."*
→ The whole field goes faintly sick. End here. A witness trustworthy
enough to indict itself. Let it land. Stop talking.

**`0` or `Esc`** — reset to silence (between rehearsal runs).

---

## Rules of the room

- **Rehearse until the keys are muscle memory.** The cue is: finish the
  spoken question, press the key. Practice the 1→2→3→approve→4→5 run a
  dozen times.
- **Don't improvise a sixth question.** There's no clip for it. If Jose
  asks his own question, answer in your own voice as the founder:
  *"It can't speak to that one yet — no client wired — but here's how it
  works."* Tex's silence stays meaningful; never let it look broken.
- **Frame it honestly, once, up front:** this is a scripted scenario, no
  client connected yet — but the hold logic, the seal, and the hash chain
  are the real system. You're not faking the product; you're showing it
  on seeded data.
- The agent (`ap-disbursement-03`) is the same in beats 2, 3, and 4 on
  purpose — it's one estate, one story.
